Why become a domain name suggester?

When you register to become a name suggester, you'll be able to suggest names for open projects. After 7 days (or sooner), the job poster must pick a winning suggestion. If that was your suggestion, you earn the bounty.

There are 3 main benefits

  1. Pay: I'm looking for name suggesters who can come up with GREAT names. If you're good at coming up with great names, you could easily earn $20+ per hour. However, there is no guarantee, since you only earn money if you win.
  2. Work when you want: You can suggest as many or as few names as you'd like, whenever you'd like.
  3. Honestly, it's fun: Some people love being creative and coming up with the perfect domain name. If that sounds like you, this might be right for you.

If you have any questions, just shoot me an email at Dale[at]namegeniuses.com, and I'll clear things up.
